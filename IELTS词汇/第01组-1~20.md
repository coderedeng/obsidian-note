# 雅思高频词汇 第1组 (1-20)

> 生成日期: 2026-04-21
> 来源: 雅思官方高频词汇库

---

## 1. Abandon
- **音标**: /əˈbændən/
- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 放弃，遗弃
- **例句**: The soldiers were forced to abandon their posts.
- **搭配**: abandon hope / abandon the idea

## 2. Abstract
- **音标**: /ˈæbstrækt/
- **词性**: adj. / n.
- **释义**: 抽象的；摘要
- **例句**: The concept of justice is quite abstract.
- **搭配**: abstract art / write an abstract

## 3. Accommodate
- **音标**: /əˈkɒmədeɪt/
- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 容纳；提供住宿；适应
- **例句**: The hotel can accommodate 500 guests.
- **搭配**: accommodate to / accommodate oneself to

## 4. Acquire
- **音标**: /əˈkwaɪər/
- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 获得，学到
- **例句**: She acquired a taste for classical music.
- **搭配**: acquire knowledge / acquire skills

## 5. Adequate
- **音标**: /ˈædɪkwət/
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 足够的，适当的
- **例句**: The supply is not adequate to meet the demand.
- **搭配**: adequate for / adequate to do sth

## 6. Advantage
- **音标**: /ədˈvɑːntɪdʒ/
- **词性**: n.
- **释义**: 优势，有利条件
- **例句**: What are the advantages of this plan?
- **搭配**: take advantage of / give sb an advantage

## 7. Allocate
- **音标**: /ˈæləkeɪt/
- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 分配，拨出
- **例句**: The government allocated funds for education.
- **搭配**: allocate resources / allocate time to

## 8. Ambiguous
- **音标**: /æmˈbɪɡjuəs/
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 模棱两可的，含糊不清的
- **例句**: His reply was ambiguous.
- **搭配**: ambiguous about / ambiguous statement

## 9. Analyze
- **音标**: /ˈænəlaɪz/
- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 分析
- **例句**: We need to analyze the data carefully.
- **搭配**: analyze the cause / analyze the impact

## 10. Anticipate
- **音标**: /ænˈtɪsɪpeɪt/
- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 预期，预料
- **例句**: We did not anticipate the problem.
- **搭配**: anticipate that / anticipate doing sth

## 11. Apparent
- **音标**: /əˈpærənt/
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 明显的；表面上的
- **例句**: It was apparent that she was upset.
- **搭配**: to all appearances / apparent reason

## 12. Applicable
- **音标**: /əˈplɪkəbl/
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 适用的，适当的
- **例句**: This rule is not applicable to children.
- **搭配**: applicable to / applicable rule

## 13. Approach
- **音标**: /əˈprəʊtʃ/
- **词性**: v. / n.
- **释义**: 接近；方法
- **例句**: A new approach to teaching is needed.
- **搭配**: approach to doing / take an approach

## 14. Appropriate
- **音标**: /əˈprəʊpriət/
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 适当的，合适的
- **例句**: Is this language appropriate for children?
- **搭配**: appropriate for / appropriate behavior

## 15. Assess
- **音标**: /əˈses/
- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 评估，评价
- **例句**: Students are assessed on their coursework.
- **搭配**: assess the impact / assess the situation

## 16. Attribute
- **音标**: /əˈtrɪbjuːt/
- **词性**: v. / n.
- **释义**: 归因于；属性
- **例句**: She attributes her success to hard work.
- **搭配**: attribute to / key attribute

## 17. Benefit
- **音标**: /ˈbenɪfɪt/
- **词性**: n. / v.
- **释义**: 好处；受益
- **例句**: The new policy will benefit everyone.
- **搭配**: benefit from / for the benefit of

## 18. Bias
- **音标**: /ˈbaɪəs/
- **词性**: n.
- **释义**: 偏见，偏向
- **例句**: The report showed a clear bias against women.
- **搭配**: gender bias / cultural bias

## 19. Capability
- **音标**: /ˌkeɪpəˈbɪləti/
- **词性**: n.
- **释义**: 能力，性能
- **例句**: She has the capability to manage the project.
- **搭配**: capability to do / military capability

## 20. Capture
- **音标**: /ˈkæptʃər/
- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: 捕获，俘获；捕捉
- **例句**: The camera captured the moment perfectly.
- **搭配**: capture the market / capture attention
